Fried Plantain

Sweet, ripe plantains fried to golden perfection. A delicious Nigerian snack that's both sweet and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe plantains
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt (optional)

Random Fact: Plantains are a member of the banana family but contain more starch and less sugar than dessert bananas!
